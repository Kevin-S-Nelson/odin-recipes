Scrambled Eggs

Description

With a little patinece and the proper pan, you too can create amazing scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 4-6 Large
  • Milk - 4-6 TBSP
  • Salt & Pepper - to taste
  • Spices/Seasonings: Parsley or other herbs - to taste
  • Cheese (Optional): Shredded chedder or mexican blend - to taste

Steps

  1. Crack eggs on a flat surface and combine in a bowl
  2. Add milk to the bowl with eggs and wisk with fork until whites and yolks are thoroughly mixed
  3. Heat a non-stick pan with butter and/or olive oil over medium heat
  4. Once the pan is warm (butter bubbles or a drop of watter sizzles) slowley add egg mixture and reduce heat to medium-low
  5. When the eggs begin to "set", gently stir with a rubber spatula
  6. Add salt, pepper, and other seasonings by gently folding the ingredinets into the eggs
  7. Continue cooking eggs while stiring, until almost all of the mixture has cooked thorough, leaving a few wet areas
  8. Remove from heat and continue to stir until the eggs are completely cooked
