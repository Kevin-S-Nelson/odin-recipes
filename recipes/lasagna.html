Lasagna

Description

Though time consuming, this famous lasagna recipe by John Chandler is well worth your time.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion - 1
  • Garlic - 2 cloves
  • Tomatoes: Crushed tomates - 1 can, tomato sauce - 2 cans, and tomato paste - 2 cans
  • Sugar - 2 TBSP
  • Spices/Seasonings: Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles - 1 box
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricota - to taste
  • Egg - 1

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
